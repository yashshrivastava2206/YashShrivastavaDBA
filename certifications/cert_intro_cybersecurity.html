About This Certification

The Introduction to Cybersecurity certification from Cisco Networking Academy provides foundational knowledge of cybersecurity concepts, exploring the cyber threat landscape, organizational security strategies, and career opportunities in the cybersecurity field. This beginner-level course serves as an entry point into the world of information security.

With cyber threats becoming increasingly prevalent and sophisticated, understanding cybersecurity basics is essential for everyone in IT. This certification introduces key security concepts and demonstrates why cybersecurity matters in today's connected world.
